Factbox: Elon Musk's Twitter polls - from stake sales to CEO decisions


A majority of Twitter users voted for billionaire Elon Musk to step down as the chief executive of the social media firm after he started a poll on Sunday evening, the latest twist in a chaotic reign following his $44 billion takeover.

The poll, which concluded with 57.5% of the votes favoring Musk step down as CEO, started after his tweet on Sunday that said: "Going forward, there will be a vote for major policy changes."
